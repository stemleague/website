Become A Mentor

Our mission is to Educate and Inspire students to Pursue STEM opportunities. This summer, we are actively looking for motivated college student mentors to help support students throughout the 2022 Developer Program. As a Program Mentor, you will be assigned to our student developers, where you will support them on learning coding concepts and provide them virtual support via virtual communication each week. Program Mentors play a vital role in our program, because you are the bridge between the students and the Developer Program, and we hope that every student comes out of the program with confidence that they can ask questions and that they belong in the STEM community.

Mentor Responsibilities

• College student or previous Developer alumni with experience in Python and/or HTML/CSS/Javascript
• Dedicate ~3-4 hours/week throughout the program duration (July 11-22 and/or July 25-August 5) to help assigned student group (student advice, review activities and lessons)
• Have access to the Internet for virtual communication
• Attend 1 hour Mentor Orientation in June
