Educate. Inspire. Pursue.

STEM League Developer Program is a virtual summer coding program that aims to bring STEM Education to students in US or Canada. The Web Development track will be hosted from July 11 to July 22, 2022 and the Data Programming track will be hosted from July 25 to August 5, 2022. Get ready for a jam-packed 2 weeks of learning through interactive coding competitions, mentorship, and a supportive student community. We invite all 6th-10th grade students who are motivated to learn coding this summer to apply. No coding experience is required.

The Application for 2022 Developer Program is now closed.

Our Global Impact

In our pilot year in Summer 2020, our team of 50 mentors taught over 300 students around the world how to code and make their own personal projects. In 2020, the Developer Program has been recognized by Microsoft, T-Mobile, University of Washington Seattle, and Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.

STEM Education Reimagined

STEM League+ is an immersive learning platform that provides students exclusive access to all of STEM League's lessons, curriculum, and activites.

Coding Tracks

Web Development

Dive into how websites are developed using HTML/CSS and Javascript with a culminating project of creating your own personal website to share at the Final Showcase.

Data Programming

Learn how to create your own program in Python to analyze datasets, create cool visualizations, and start your own data science project!

Eligiblity

  • Current 6th-10th grade student in the USA or Canada
  • Access to own laptop and Internet for video/audio call via Zoom/Discord
  • Dedicate 2 weeks of the program to actively learn and engage in mentor meetups and program activities

Frequently Asked Questions

Can students outside of US or Canada apply?
No, due to time zones differences we cannot accomodate students outside of US/Canada.

Is this a virtual program?
Yes, it will be virtual learning including online lessons and virtual activites.

What is the age requirement to apply?
We are accepting students from grades 6-10 (rising 7th/11th graders are eligible).

How long is this program?
The duration of this program is 2 weeks.

What will I get out of this program? Why should I learn to code?
This program is your opportunity to learn coding skills to build your own personal projects and also your chance to gain mentorship from Program Mentors virtually. We want you to take what you learn and leverage yourself in your future career!

Why are lessons pre-recorded?
The instructors are working during the week so they are unable to accomodate in-person lessons. However, we want to accomodate all students of different learning levels so we will have Program Mentors who will be assigned a mentee group to work together on a weekly basis.
